Welcome User!
This is an advanced screening tool built for shortlisting profiles against your firm’s Job Roles.

Using this tool, you can –
1. Parse, extract and edit relevant skills, educational qualifications, years of experience from the Job Descriptions.

2. Fetch Resumes from Firm’s Email Address and Naukri Database and shortlist relevant profiles based on scores received against the JDs.



In this section, you can create a new JD by choosing the option ‘Create New JD’ and put relevant information and then click ‘Create JD’ button.
If you want to parse your existing JDs, you can click the button ‘Parse Existing JDs’.
